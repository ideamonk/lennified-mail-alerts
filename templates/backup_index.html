What is Lenny?

Lenny is a simple gmail notification tool. If your twitter account is sms enabled, it alerts you whenever you get a new mail in your inbox.


In the movie Toy Story, Lenny is a pair of binoculars with feet. He is talkative when he warns the toys what he sees on his watch. So, now you see the co-relation!
